Subject: M. Farhan Hamim // Founder & CEO

01 THE AWAKENING

Born on January 15, 2011, in Bangladesh, my journey didn't start with a plan, but with a question. I entered the tech world simply because I have a deep hunger to learn new things.

I found that coding isn't just a skill—it's an infinite universe. Every line of code I write is a step further into a world where anything is possible.

02 MY NINDŌ

I've always worked on my own way. Sometimes it doesn't match what others expect, but I don't see that as a flaw—it's my unique point.

My life resonates with Naruto and Gaara. Like them, I faced a path where I had to define myself. I don't follow the crowd; I build the road.

03 THE EMPIRE BUILDER

I believe in efficiency. Every project I take on follows a simple Python-inspired logic: if profit == True: do_work().

This mindset led me to found PSBDx. I'm not just a reseller; I'm the architect. I built the plugins and the systems that allow me to provide premium subscriptions to Bangladesh at the best prices.

04 GIVING BACK

Even as a CEO, I never forget the community. I build custom Discord bots and plugins for free because I believe in the power of open source.

Sharing knowledge is how we all grow. Most of my projects are open for the world to see on GitHub, because a true leader lifts others up.
